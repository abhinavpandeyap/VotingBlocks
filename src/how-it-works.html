How does VotingBlocks work?

by Election Commission of India

Posted on June 1, 2022 at 12:00 PM

Standing in the queue and waiting for your turn to cast vote is a part of a history now.

Election Commission of India proposes a new system of voting where everything is transparent and no question of tampering the voting machine as it doesn't exists in first place.

VotingBlocks is the upcoming way of casting vote where all the votes will be stored in the Blockchain, which makes the system tamper proof and fully transparent.

This is still under various trials! Please expect few bugs.

How to use?

1. Install MetaMask Browser Extension.

2. Install MetaMask legacy Web3 Browser Extension.

3. Make sure that the Ethereum Account which you've been assigned is imported into MetaMask.

4. Visit the Cast Vote Page and cast your vote
